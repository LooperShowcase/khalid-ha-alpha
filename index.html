Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz (German: [mɛɐ̯ˈtseːdəsˌbɛnts, -dɛs-]),[6][7] commonly referred to as Mercedes, is a German automotive luxury brand (and subsidiary – as Mercedes-Benz AG – of Daimler AG) headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.[1] Mercedes-Benz produces luxury vehicles and commercial vehicles.[note 2] The first Mercedes-Benz brand name vehicles were produced in 1926. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the largest seller of premium vehicles in the world, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars.[8]
